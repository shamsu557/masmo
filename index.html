Founder of MASMO

Shitu Madaki Gari

Maliya Shitu is a Advisor on media to DSP Barau I jibrin and a visionary leader and the driving force behind the **Maliya Shitu Media Organization (MASMO)**. With a passion for political development and media excellence, he has dedicated his efforts to promoting the leadership and achievements of **Senator Barau I. Jibrin**, ensuring that his vision for progress reaches the people.
