Distinguished Senator Barau I. Jibrin

DSP Barau I. Jibrin

Senator Barau I. Jibrin, the Deputy Senate President of Nigeria, is a dedicated leader committed to national development, legislative excellence, and the empowerment of the people. Through his unwavering dedication to public service, he has spearheaded numerous initiatives focused on infrastructure, education, and economic growth.

His leadership continues to inspire progress, fostering a governance system that prioritizes the needs of the people and ensures sustainable development for all.
