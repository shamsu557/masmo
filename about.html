About Us

Maliya Shitu Media Organization (MASMO) is a dedicated media platform established to **champion the political vision, leadership, and achievements** of Distinguished Senator Barau I. Jibrin, the Deputy Senate President of Nigeria. As a voice for progress, MASMO is committed to bridging the gap between leadership and the people by effectively communicating policies, programs, and developmental strides.

At MASMO, we believe in **the power of media as a tool for awareness, education, and engagement**. Through strategic media campaigns, grassroots outreach, and digital storytelling, we highlight **Senator Barau I. Jibrin’s contributions to governance, economic growth, and national unity**. Our mission is to **empower citizens with accurate information**, foster political awareness, and strengthen the bond between leadership and the electorate.

With a commitment to **integrity, inclusivity, and transparency**, MASMO serves as a dynamic force in shaping public discourse, ensuring that the senator’s impact is recognized at the local, national, and global levels. We are more than a media organization; we are a movement for progress.

Our Vision

To be a leading media organization that strengthens political awareness, fosters unity, and amplifies the impact of progressive leadership in Nigeria.

Our Mission

Our mission is to utilize media as a tool for transparency, education, and engagement, ensuring that the policies and achievements of Senator Barau I. Jibrin reach the grassroots and beyond. We strive to empower citizens with accurate information, promote civic responsibility, and contribute to national development.

State Key Executives

The MASMO State Executive Council consists of dedicated individuals who oversee the strategic operations of the organization. Each member plays a crucial role in ensuring smooth coordination, media engagement, and effective grassroots mobilization.

The executives include the Chairman, Vice Chairman, Secretary, and other key officials responsible for various departments such as media, youth affairs, women’s mobilization, and public relations. Their collective efforts contribute to the success of MASMO in amplifying the vision and leadership of Senator Barau I. Jibrin across Kano State and beyond.

Together, they drive MASMO’s mission with passion, dedication, and teamwork.
